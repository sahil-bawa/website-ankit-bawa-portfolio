WHAT TO PACK?

You should know what to carry!

  • Ankit Bawa
  • Experience

My love for travel is endless, and my love for hiking has just begun. I started hiking back in 2017 but grew my love for it over a period of time. Since then, I have done 8-9 expeditions which involves treks like Kedarkantha, Dayara Bugyal, Laka Glacier. Every time I step my foot on a mountain, I JUST FEEL ALIVE.

The biggest challenge I face back in the days was about the appropriate gear, for what to carry and what not to. Which is the perfect bag for me? Should I carry a shoulder bag on my trek? How many tshirts should I keep? Is this much water enough for a trek? How many undergarments will I need?

Keeping my experiences and my journey in mind, I came up with this video. A one stop solution for everyone who comes across these questions. For everyone who is just starting and for everyone who never knew the quantity they should carry while trekking.

Do your research and find out what is available on the ground and at what cost. Ask the tour company you're going with or reach out to other independent travelers who've experienced the same trek. When you land on the ground, shop around for the best price.
