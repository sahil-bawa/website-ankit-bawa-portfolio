THE SPITI CIRCUIT

Have some perspective while traveling

  • Ankit Bawa
  • Photography

One reason I love capturing my life and making these videos is to showcase my perspective, how I see things, how different everything can be, what can you miss when not being fully into the moment. You have to let go your inner thoughts and the things that are pulling you down to enjoy the present moment.

In this video, I showcase my first Roadtrip with friends, to Spiti Valley. Long winding roads and valleys that present unforgettable glimpses of cold desert and snow-crowned mountains welcome you when you set foot into Spiti Valley. Bordered on all sides by the Himalayas

While being on the road, thrilled with experiences, experiencing new food, new culture, I made sure to capture all of it, to make a documented series to showcase everything with my perspective. One thing to keep in mind is to showcase your perspective, and not copying.

Always remember, we do not travel to seek new landscapes, we travel to seek new eyes, to see the unseen. To experience things you thought never existed. To experience self discovery, you have to let go to live.
